Our Story

A Celebration of Nigerian Heritage

Born from a deep reverence for the Ìjẹ̀bú people's culinary traditions, Ìjẹ̀bú Spice Lounge is Lagos' most distinguished Nigerian fine dining experience. We honour the stories, spices, and soul of a cuisine that has nourished generations.

Nestled in the heart of Victoria Island, our lounge blends authentic flavours with contemporary elegance — every dish a love letter to Nigeria's rich cultural tapestry, every evening an unforgettable journey.

Meet the Team

The Culinary Masters

Chef Tunde Adebayo
Executive Chef
18 years mastering the art of Yoruba cuisine. Trained in Paris, soul remains in Lagos. Architect of our signature menu.
Chef Amaka Okafor
Head of Soups & Stews
An Anambra native bringing the soul of Eastern Nigerian soups — her Egusi is legendary in every room she enters.
Chef Chinedu Eze
Grill & Small Chops Master
The Suya whisperer of Victoria Island. His charcoal-kissed creations turn street food into fine dining poetry.

Pastry & Drinks Artisan
